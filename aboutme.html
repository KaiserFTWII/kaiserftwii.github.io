About Me

A more formal description of who I am and why I'm interested in Civil Engineering Software

To continue where I left off from the home page, I am, specifically, in my third year of study at the University of Toronto, pursuing a Bachelor of Applied Science and Engineering, with a major in Civil Engineering. Ever since high school, I was interested in the role of coding in an advancing society, especially regarding artifical intelligence and more intelligent cities. My focus shifted towards civil engineering as I became more exposed to the industry. I started noticing more construction projects around my area such as condo development going on nearby. I also started to watch more programs about residential properties and saw how houses can be modelled in 3D softwares before a single piece of dirt is excavated. These models became more intiguing and I also developed a bit of interest in construction when looking at such models.

My curiosity led me to where I am today. Over the past few years, I have accumulated a number of software skills which can be applied to the industry and have expanded my horizons reagrding software in civil engineering. I am eager to expand them further and excited to see what the future hold for me!
